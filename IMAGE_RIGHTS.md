# Product Image Rights Workflow

parapharmacie.me does not reuse competitor product photos, marketplace images, search-result images, or brand website packshots unless rights are clearly granted for ecommerce use.

## Current Status

- Product image folder: `assets/products/`
- Active storefront images: generated owned category fallback `.webp` assets.
- Catalog coverage: 93 verified products currently use generated fallback visuals.
- Product-photo approval count: 0 products.
- Products still requiring approved packshots: 93 products.
- `imageNeedsReview` remains `true` for every product until an owned, distributor-supplied, licensed, or brand-approved packshot is documented.
- No third-party product photos have been downloaded or committed.
- No remote product images are used in the local catalog.

## Rights Review Notes

Official brand sites were checked for representative catalog brands. Their public legal terms generally reserve photographs, images, trademarks, and site content unless there is explicit written authorization or a narrow personal/private/press-use exception. Because these terms do not grant general ecommerce reuse rights to parapharmacie.me, their product photos were not copied into the repository.

Reference terms checked:

- Uriage legal notice and sales terms: `https://www.uriage.fr/mentions-legales`, `https://www.uriage.fr/cgv`
- La Roche-Posay terms: `https://www.laroche-posay.fr/service-client/conditions-d%E2%80%99utilisation-du-site/customer-service-terms-and-conditions.html`
- Bioderma intellectual property notice: `https://www.bioderma.bg/prava-na-intelektualna-sobstvenost`
- NUXE sales terms / intellectual property: `https://fr.nuxe.com/pages/conditions-generales-de-vente`
- Caudalie legal statement / sales terms: `https://fr.caudalie.com/legal-statement-fr`, `https://fr.caudalie.com/cgv`
- SVR terms: `https://ma.svr.com/pages/conditions-generales-de-vente`

## Approved Production Image Sources

- Original product photos produced by Pharmacie Tawfiq/parapharmacie.me.
- Brand or distributor assets with written approval for ecommerce use by parapharmacie.me.
- Public-domain or explicitly commercial-use licensed assets, with license evidence retained.
- CDN-hosted assets only when the license and hotlink/CDN terms allow that workflow.

## Needs Review

- Product photos from Moroccan competitor websites.
- Marketplace images from retailers, aggregators, or search results.
- Official brand packshots without written ecommerce usage permission.
- Remote image URLs used temporarily during merchandising.

## Replacement Steps

1. Export approved product packshots as optimized `.webp`.
2. Store files in `assets/products/` with stable slug-based names.
3. Update the product `image` field to the approved `.webp` path.
4. Set `imageSource`, `imageRightsStatus`, and `imageReplacementNote`.
5. Change `imageNeedsReview` to `false` only after usage rights are documented.
6. Run `npm run validate:catalog`, `npm run lint`, and `npm run build`.
